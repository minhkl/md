Privacy Policy

How we use information about you

Information that is collected when you visit MechanicDesk's website

When you visit MechanicDesk's site, details such as your IP address, browser type, time and length of visit, may be recorded. This is for website development purposes and analytics of the website only. If you decide to register or enquire about MechanicDesk, your details that you provide such as your password, address, email address and phone number will be stored on MechanicDesk servers.

Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the website.

How the information is used

The information is used to enhance the visitor's experience when using the website. MechanicDesk undertakes that it will not sell, rent or lease email addresses to third parties. We may use your information to inform you of news of our services, in this case we will provide you with the opportunity to opt out of future updates from us.

Advertising tracking

Cookies may be used by third party advertising companies for tracking purposes. Cookies are a small piece of data that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences and activity to be recorded. You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website. Google, as a third party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to visitors based on their visit to sites they visit on the Internet. Website visitors may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

Information that is collected when you use MechanicDesk software

MechanicDesk is committed to protecting your privacy and your data. Any information collected by MechanicDesk will not be transferred to or shared with any third party without your consent. MechanicDesk uses safe, secure technologies to protect your personal information.

We take all reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access, modification or disclosure. Your data is stored on secure servers that have SSL Certificates issued by leading certificate authorities Comodo. All data transferred between You and MechanicDesk is encrypted.

However, the Internet is not in itself a secure environment and we cannot give an absolute assurance that Your information will be secure at all times. Transmission of information over the Internet is at Your own risk and You should only enter, or instruct the entering of, personal information to the Service within a secure environment.

We will advise You at the first reasonable opportunity upon discovering or being advised of a security breach where Your personal information is lost, stolen, accessed, used, disclosed, copied, modified, or disposed of by any unauthorised persons or in any unauthorised manner.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

MechanicDesk reserves the right to make amendments to this Privacy Policy at any time, and will notify you via your registered email address if and when such ammendments occur.

Contacting us

MechanicDesk welcomes your comments regarding this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy and would like further information, please contact us.

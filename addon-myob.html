Seamless integration with MYOB for a complete business solution


MYOB and MechanicDesk together form an elegant solution, keeping you in complete control of your business.

All your stocks, customers, suppliers, invoices, and payments will be transferred between MechanicDesk and MYOB automatically, keeping all your financial reports up to date.

Connect these apps today to see a noticeable difference in time spent on your accounts.

  • MYOB displays your realtime cash balances, compare live data to your recent jobs, incomes and expenses.
  • Sales records are cleverly matched to your customer records in MYOB
  • Automatically match products ordered with items listed in MYOB


What is MYOB?


MYOB is easy to use online accounting software that’s designed specifically for small businesses.

  • It's all online, so you can work when and where you want to. Just login with your PC, Mac or mobile.
  • Your bank statements are automatically imported and categorized, letting you see your cashflow in real-time.
  • Invite your team and work together on financials. You can collaborate over your up-to-date numbers.
  • MYOB has all you need to run your business – including invoicing, paying bills, sales tax returns, reporting and much more.

Find out more or try MYOB Accounting Software for free.


How to connect


  • Login to MechanicDesk
  • Go to Settings
  • Go to Accounting Integration
  • Follow the step by step process to connect to MYOB - and you're flying!

Reporting


  • We support Income, Expense, Work in Progress, Employee, Marketing and Stock report categories, each with their own set of reports.


Know your business inside out

MechanicDesk provides a comprehensive set of reports to help you better understand your business. The common reports such as Sales, Purchases, Invoices, Bills, Stock Value, etc reports are available. In addition to that, MechanicDesk also supports more specialised reports for Employee Productivity and Efficiency, Income by Job Type, Commission, etc. All this aims to help you identify what is driving your workshop while any areas of deficiency can be more easily found and addressed.
